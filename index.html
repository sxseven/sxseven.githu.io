数论

莫比乌斯反演

$$
前置知识\
\sum{d|n}:对n的所有正因子求和\
F(n)=\sum{d|n}f(d)\
=>f(n)=\sum{d|n}\mu(d)F(\frac{n}{d})\bigstar\
=\sum{d|n}\mu(d)F(\frac{n}{d})\
=\sum{d|n}\mu(d)\sum{k|\frac{n}{d}}f(k)\
=\sum{k|n}f(k)\sum{d|\frac{n}{k}}\mu(d)\
\mu(n)\
rest\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad \mu(n)=0\
n=1\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad \mu(n)=1\
n=p{1}p{2}…p{k}\qquad \mu(n)=(-1)^k\
$$

例2 (洛谷p3768):给出一个n和p,求:
$$
(\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{n}ijgcd(i,j)))%p
$$
把$$gcd(i,j)$$提出来:
$$
\sum_{d=1}^{n}d\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{n}ij[gcd(i,j)==d]
$$
再提出d:
$$
(\sum_{d=1}^{n}d^3\sum_{i=1}^{n/d}\sum_{j=1}^{n/d}ij[gcd(i,j)==1]
$$
莫比乌斯反演后:
$$
\sum_{d=1}^{n}d^3\sum_{i=1}^{n/d}\mu(i)i^2(1+2+…+[\frac{n}{id}])^2
$$
设$$sum(x)=1+2+3+…+x$$,

令T=id,则:
$$
\sum_{T=1}^{n}sum(\frac{n}{T})^2\sum_{d|T}d^3\frac{T^2}{d}\mu(\frac{T}{d})\
$$
约去d:
$$
\sum_{T=1}^{n}sum(\frac{n}{T})^2T^2\sum_{d|T}d\mu(\frac{T}{d})
$$
